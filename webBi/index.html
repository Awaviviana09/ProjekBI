How To update website?

To update a website, you generally need to have access to the website's content management system (CMS) or code. Depending on the type of website you have, you may be able to update content, such as text and images, directly through the CMS. Alternatively, you may need to update the code of your website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. If you're not familiar with website development, it's often best to work with a professional web development team who can handle updates for you. They can ensure that your updates are made correctly and that your website continues to function properly after the updates are complete.
